## 4. CALM v1.1 — EEG Zoom Law

**Title:** CALM v1.1: EEG Zoom Law Dataset and Minimal Evaluator  
**Type:** Dataset  
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17627574   

CALM (Cognitive Alphabet for Latent Microstates) v1.1 proposes a **zoom law** in resting-state EEG:

- Refining one key latent state (S1) into finer substates produces a **reliable gain** in predictive / compression metrics  
- This gain is measured across subjects and compared with non-hierarchical refinements  

The dataset includes:

- `zoom_S1_summary.json` — summary statistics for the zoom effect  
- `zoom_S1_subjects.csv` — per-subject metrics  
- `calm_eval_min.py` — a minimal evaluator to test the zoom law on new EEG datasets  

Recommended use: your **first hard cortical law**—a compact EEG language that others can directly attack or extend.
